### **Guide**

- **Availability is king.** It sits on the main line because "Can I get this *right now*?" is the first question in any narrative interaction.
- **Enthusiasm matters.** A blacksmith might *do* armor repairs, but if they're reluctant or exhausted by the work, that creates frictionâ€”and story.
- **Cost is relative.** *Affordable*, *Premium*, and *Negotiable* are intentionally vague. Exact prices aren't the point; the *feel* of the transaction is.
- **Descriptions stay short.** 1-2 sentences. Just enough to ground the category and hint at character or quality.
