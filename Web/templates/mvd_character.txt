### **Guide**

- **AgeCategory** uses four tiers: *Youth* (immature), *Younger* (adult), *Older* (middle-aged+), *Elder* (venerable). This keeps age relative and flexible.
- **FeatureRelationship** is crucialâ€”it ties the character to the Feature and defines their stake. *Proprietor*, *Daughter*, *Unrelated Apprentice*, *Hired Hand*, *Tenant*, etc.
- **Traits** should be punchy and evocative. Three personality keywords + one backstory hook. Keep it tight.
- **Remark** is the character's voice or motivation in a single line. It should feel like something they'd actually say or think.
- **Companion OwnerID** must use the full token (e.g., `â‘¡â“ğŸ‘¤3`) to link the animal to its owner. This keeps cross-referencing clean.
- **Companions** can be working animals (*Draft Horse*, *Guard Dog*), familiars (*Fernshadow*, *Raven*), or livestock (*Chickens*, *Goats*). The **Purpose** field clarifies their role.
